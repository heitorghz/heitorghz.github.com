Physics: Motion Review Flashcards

What does URM stand for in physics?
Uniform Rectilinear Motion?
What is Uniform Rectilinear Motion?
It is motion in a straight line with constant speed.
Does the object speed up or slow down in URM?
No, the speed stays constant all the time.
What does UARM stand for in physics?
Uniformly Accelerated Rectilinear Motion.
What is Uniformly Accelerated Rectilinear Motion?
It is motion in a straight line where the speed changes at a constant rate.
Does the acceleration change in UARM?
No, the acceleration is always constant.
How can you tell if a motion is URM or UARM?
If the speed stays constant, it’s URM. If the speed changes, it’s UARM.
What happens if the object has positive acceleration?
It moves faster over time and can change direction.
What happens if the object has negative acceleration?
It gets slower and slower and can change direction.
