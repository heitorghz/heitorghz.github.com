Local Championship Seminals: Brasiliense Vs Gama

Long awaited match between Gama and Brasiliense will happen in this Saturday (03/15) at 4:00 PM

This weekend, the game will take place at the Bezerrão stadium, the winner will secure a position in the finals of the Candangão, the local soccer championship.

Specialists say: The Brasiliense team is playing in an Away Ground while Gama's team is going to be playing in their Home Ground. Making this game quite difficult to the visiting team.

The score between the two teams is 14 wins for Gama, 3 draws and only 3 wins for the Brasiliense team, making it clear that one side is much more likely to win than the other.
